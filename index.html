About
us

In 2016, The Gavalis was established Abhishek Gavali, where it opened up enormous opportunities in the biggest and most challenging dairy market in the world to position it as the most preferred brand in India. The traditional and cultural experience of Anik and international expertise of Lactalis provided a unique combination, leading to a success story to be talked by generations to come.

Legal Compliance

We strictly ensure to be compliant with all the legal norms, whether it is for the products or brands in the market.We believe in the Philosophy of “Consumer First” We understand its importance and that is why we guarantee its fulfilment.

Quality at the best price

Being in a commodity & dairy market, we ensure our products are not only the healthiest and tastiest but also affordable for one and all. Quality is not something we strive for, it’s something we guarantee with consistency.

Food Safety Guarantee

We believe in the Philosophy of “Consumer First”. As a part of our Corporate Responsibility, we ensure that food safety is on our top priority. All our plants have FSSAI Licenses certifying a commitment towards food safety and dependability.
